# Cyber Quest
Cyber-Quest is an interactive game designed for elementary school students. The aim is to use stories and games to raise children's awareness and teach them how to behave on the internet, what to watch out for, what to avoid, and some good practices for staying safe. Our goal is for this to be a tool for teachers, who will use it in their computer science classes.

---
## Level 1
### Rules for creating a secure and strong password

The aim of this level is to teach children how to create a strong password for their accounts. What rules should they follow, what are some good practices, and what should they avoid adding? In this level, they will learn all the basic rules and will be asked to think of additional solutions that provide greater security. At the same time, through the story, they learn that they should not open links that come from unknown senders or that they are not sure about.
